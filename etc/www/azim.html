Plan view:

    Enter zoom factor, 1.0 is normal.

Cross-section:

    Enter azimuth of cross-section, e.g. 90 for west to east, 360 for south to north.

All-sky:

    Enter azimuth and type of all-sky plot with one of these values (without the quotes)

    • "360p" - Polar (fish-eye) view where north is up
    • "180p" - Polar (fish-eye) view where south is up
    • "360pr" - Polar (fish-eye) view where north is up and image is flipped from left to right
    • "180pr" - Polar (fish-eye) view where south is up and image is flipped from left to right
    • "360c" - Cylindrical panoramic 360 degree view where north is centered
    • "180c" - Cylindrical panoramic 360 degree view where south is centered
    • other values - Polar (fish-eye) view where north is up and image is flipped from left to right (default setting)
