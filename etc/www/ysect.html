Plan view:

Enter Y for center point. Range is 0.0 to 1.0, 0.5 is normal.

Cross-section:

Enter Y for waypoint (0.0 <= y < 1.0) OR J for waypoint in gridpoints (e.g. 50).

For longitude append with the letter "l" (e.g. -105.0l)

Sounding or All-Sky:

Enter J for sounding location in gridpoints (e.g. 50)

For longitude append with the letter "l" (e.g. -105.0l)
